What are Badges? and are they worth it?

There are many sites on the internet that provide courses and tutorials in software development. The hard part for a new developer is finding the right ones for you.

Many of the larger technology firms offer Badge courses. These courses are often free and are professionally conducted. They reinforce the information they teach, and once completed provide you with a Badge that can be posted onto you cv and LinkedIn page.

Below are some of the courses available:

Cognitive Class (IBM)

Not only does IBM offer individual courses, but also actual learning paths.

These are great for people new to the industry, who might have a career in mind, but are unsure of how to get there.

They are also a great way to "taste test" the type of work you think you might want to get into
